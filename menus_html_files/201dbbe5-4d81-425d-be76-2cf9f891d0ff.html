We transport our guests, allowing them to experience the tastes and vibe of France without leaving the city.

Welcome to the Neighborhood. You don’t have to live in our community to be treated like a neighbor. Located on the Upper West Side in Manhattan, Café du Soleil celebrates the eclectic mix of people who love our comfort-French cuisine. Our family loves welcoming yours, bringing you into our ‘home’, and sharing our passion for fine food. It’s amazing how quickly the pressures of the day disappear once you’ve joined us at Café du Soleil. This is where pretension falls away and family comes together.

The Best of France in NYC. You feel it as soon as you see it: a casual elegance reminiscent of so many French Bistros that dot the Mediterranean landscape. With French blues and kisses of yellow, big brass-framed mirrors, and a light and airy ambiance, Café du Soleil is a French family-owned bistro where good friends and good food are plentiful.

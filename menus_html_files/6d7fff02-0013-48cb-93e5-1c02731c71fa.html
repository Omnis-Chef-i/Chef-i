Irashaimase!

Established in 2011, HinoMaru Ramen is humbled to be the first ramen-ya to open its doors in Astoria. HinoMaru 日の丸 loosely translates to the “circle of the sun”.  We specialize in seven regional Japanese ramen–from Sapporo in the north to Kagoshima in the south.

All of our broths are house-made.  The tonkotsu bone broth is our signature preparation.  To render as much flavor, fat, and protein as possible, we keep the broth on a rolling boil for 17 hours.  We slowly reduced the broth at decreasing temperatures to release the complex bone marrowy flavors.   For us, quality ramen starts and ends with our broths!

Please see our menu below for a few of our personal favorites.

We look forward to seeing you soon!

Arigato gozaimashita!

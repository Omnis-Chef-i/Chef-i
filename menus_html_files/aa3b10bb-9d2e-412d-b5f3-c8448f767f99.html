THE JEWEL OF BRYANT PARK

Parisian-style dining situated behind the landmark New York Public Library, Bryant Park serves as a stunning backdrop for Bryant Park Grill. The restaurant’s sophisticated dining room is perfect for a business lunch or romantic dining. Enjoy a festive party under our heated holiday tent in the winter, while seasonal patio & roof dining is sure to make your summer evenings magical. Choose from our extensive whiskey selection or sip classic cocktails at the rooftop bar. The Garden and Café offer alfresco dining in a delightful and unexpected oasis at the center of the world’s most spectacular city.

Sustainability

At Bryant Park Grill, sustainability is a passion and we are constantly looking for new ways to operate our business in a more eco-friendly manner. We go the extra mile to source products from local vendors to ensure freshness and support our community. We are dedicated to buying and serving seafood from suppliers who are highly reputable and transparent in their business practices. Lobster Place and Samuels & Sons are two of our most trusted suppliers and have both gone through some of the most rigorous food safety and sustainability certification processes in the industry. Both have strong ties and meet standards set by the Monterey Bay Aquarium Seafood Watch. Our beverage program features organic, biodynamic, and locally sourced wines along with small batch and locally produced spirits.

We replaced plastic straws in 2018 with biodegradable options, aligning with our commitment to reducing single-use plastic. Our paper takeout containers are made from recycled materials. We have adopted NYC Waste Management rules and regulations for waste disposal, composting, and recycling. Our strong partnership with Ecolab for all our chemical and ware washing needs allows us to work with a company dedicated to protecting the environment, addressing social concerns, and adhering to responsible policies and practices. We partner with distributors who donate and support City Harvest including:

  • I. Halper Paper & Supplies Inc.
  • Empire Merchants
  • Baldor Specialty Food

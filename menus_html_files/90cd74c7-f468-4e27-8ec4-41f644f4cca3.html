Down the Alleyway...

In the fall of 2004, we happened upon a perfect cozy space at the end of an unused alley on the Lower East Side, off the Bowery, and Freemans Restaurant was born. 

The idea of the restaurant was to make a rugged clandestine colonial American Tavern.

The cuisine was also imagined to early American: Simple, Rustic, and inspired by Old World traditions.

Reservation Policy

Walk-ins for small parties are encouraged at any time.

A portion of seating is held for walk-in guests.  We seat only completed parties on a first come, first served basis.

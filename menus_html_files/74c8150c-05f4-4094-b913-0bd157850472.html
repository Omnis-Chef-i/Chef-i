Gabriel’s Gate Menu

Delicious Delights Await You

Gabriel’s Gate invites you to indulge in a wide range of appetizers that will tantalize Gabriel’s Gate’s taste buds. From Gabriel’s Gate’s famous Buffalo chicken wings, perfectly seasoned and served with Gabriel’s Gate’s choice of sauce, to Gabriel’s Gate’s crispy calamari with a tangy aioli dip, the restaurant’s appetizers are sure to kickstart your dining experience.

For those seeking a lighter option, Gabriel’s Gate’s menu offers a variety of fresh and vibrant salads. Enjoy the classic Caesar salad with grilled shrimp or relish the combination of mixed greens, creamy goat cheese, and candied walnuts tossed in the restaurant’s flavorful balsamic vinaigrette.

Gabriel’s Gate sandwiches are crafted with care, using the finest ingredients to create a satisfying meal. Sink your teeth into Gabriel’s Gate’s classic Reuben sandwich, piled high with tender corned beef, sauerkraut, Swiss cheese, and Russian dressing. Alternatively, try Gabriel’s Gate’s chicken caprese sandwich, featuring succulent grilled chicken, fresh mozzarella, juicy tomatoes, and basil pesto.

When it comes to Gabriel’s Gate’s entrees, prepare to be impressed. Dive into Gabriel’s Gate’s juicy grilled ribeye steak, served with creamy mashed potatoes and grilled asparagus, or savor the delicate flavors of Gabriel’s Gate’s perfectly seared Atlantic salmon, accompanied by a lemon butter sauce and a side of wild rice.

To end Gabriel’s Gate ‘s meal on a sweet note, the restaurant’s desserts are a must-try. Delight in the velvety smoothness of Gabriel’s Gate ‘s New York cheesecake, topped with your choice of raspberry or chocolate sauce, or indulge in Gabriel’s Gate ‘s warm chocolate lava cake, oozing with rich molten chocolate and served with a scoop of homemade vanilla ice cream.

Flavors That Mesmerize Instantly

Gabriel’s Gate takes immense pride in Gabriel’s Gate’s renowned Buffalo-style wings, which have made the restaurant a beloved establishment in Buffalo, New York since 1974. Over the years, Gabriel’s Gate’s wings have garnered consistent acclaim from both critics and diners, establishing the restaurant as a go-to destination for wing enthusiasts.

Gabriel’s Gate ‘s wings are crafted using only the finest ingredients, starting with fresh, never-frozen chicken wings. Each wing is carefully hand-cut to ensure optimal size and tenderness. To achieve that irresistible crispy exterior, Gabriel’s Gate ‘s wings undergo a meticulous double-frying process that guarantees a satisfying crunch with every bite.

The true magic lies in Gabriel’s Gate’s signature sauces, which elevate the restaurant’s wings to new heights of flavor. The restaurant’s talented chefs have perfected a range of sauces that cater to every palate. Whether you crave the classic tangy Buffalo sauce, the smoky heat of the restaurant’s barbecue sauce, or the sweet and spicy kick of the restaurant’s honey mustard sauce, the restaurant has a sauce to satisfy every taste bud.

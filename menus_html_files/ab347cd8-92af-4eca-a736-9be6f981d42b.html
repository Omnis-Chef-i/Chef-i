ABOUT US

Where Good Times and Great Drinks Meet

At Cozy Soup & Burger, we believe comfort food should feel like a warm hug. Nestled in the heart of the city, weve been serving up homestyle soups and juicy, made-to-order burgers for folks who crave a little coziness in their day.

Whether youre stopping by for a quick lunch, a late-night bite, or a slow, satisfying dinner, our kitchen is always cooking something hearty and delicious. From our rich, simmered-all-day soups to our stacked burgers on toasted buns, every dish is crafted with careand a whole lot of flavor.

We're not just about foodwe're about feeling at home. Cozy Soup & Burger is a place where regulars are family, and newcomers are always welcome.

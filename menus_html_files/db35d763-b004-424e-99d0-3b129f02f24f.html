For larger parties, we offer a family-style prix fixe menu, which includes a selection of three antipasti followed by four varieties of pizza (we'll send as many as you can eat!), and tiramisu for dessert. All dishes are served family-style, shared around the table. An unlimited beer & wine package can be added for an additional $30 per guest for up to two hours.

Reservations for large parties are accepted for lunch and dinner. We can accommodate up to 24 guests in our East Village pizzeria, and up to 30 guests in Williamsburg. Please email info@motorinopizza.com to check availability. We ask that you make your menu selections when you confirm your reservation.

Parties of 12+ guests will be asked to use this menu; tables of 6-11 are welcome to choose it as well.

For private events, please visit our Private Parties page.

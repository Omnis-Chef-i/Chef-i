About Restaurant

Welcome to Cardamom Indian Cuisine in Sunnyside, NY – where authentic Indian flavors meet warm hospitality. Whether you're craving a quick bite, a comforting meal, or a full dining experience, we offer dine-in, takeaway, and delivery options. Our menu includes halal, vegan, vegetarian, and healthy dishes, along with a selection of beer, wine, and coffee. Enjoy our cozy setting with table service, Wi-Fi, and family-friendly seating. From lunch to dinner, we bring you fresh ingredients and bold spices in every bite. Come experience the true taste of India right here in Sunnyside!

Upper West Side


Tarallucci e Vino Upper West Side is a neighborhood favorite restaurant known for expertly crafted home-made pasta, pastries and authentic italian food right near Central Park.

Our outdoor seating and cozy interior make this the perfect spot for a quick breakfast, elegant lunches, relaxed dinners or lazy weekend brunch. 

Visit our heated outdoor patio, enjoy a glass of wine indoors or we can deliver your order right to your door!

OUR LOCATION

Craving for the best italian food in the Upper West Side? Look no more. Our restaurant is located at 475 Columbus Avenue, open all day.

Hours: Sunday- Monday - 8am - 10.30pm

Friday- Saturday 8am - 11pm

Happy Hour is available at the bar from 4pm to 6 pm!

Contact us: info@taralluccievino.net

Phone: (212) 362 5454
Follow us: @taralluccinyc
Tarallucci e Vino Upper West Side is conveniently located right by the American Museum of Natural History and Central Park.

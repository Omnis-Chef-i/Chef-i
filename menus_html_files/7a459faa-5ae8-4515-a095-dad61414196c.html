    About Us

    Arden is the first restaurant from veterans Scott Dansby and Mark Mata. Mark and Scott first met at the East Village Mermaid Inn in 2012, where their partnership in running the restaurant fostered a kinship focused on great service, a fun inventive menu, and memorable experiences for their guests and staff alike. They are excited to bring this passion to their new project, Arden.

    Mark started his career at Union Square Cafe in 2004 and has worked at  BiCE Cucina, The Writing Room, & August. Scott has worked at Five & Ten, Smith's, JG Melon, & Market Table.

    Arden is located in the heart of Crown Heights Brooklyn near the Brooklyn Botanical Garden, Brooklyn Museum, and Prospect Park. Our locally-focused globally conscious menu changes with the season and highlights our wood fire grill. We have a full bar with an inventive cocktail menu and a fun wine list.
